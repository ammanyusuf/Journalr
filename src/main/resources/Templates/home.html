Journalr

This is the Journalr Web Application. It's purpose is to create a platform to manage to the interaction between Authors, Editors and Reviewers of a larger Journal collection. Authors write and submit their Journals, get responses and updates from the Editors and Reviewers and more! Login with your account to get started!

Developed by: Amman Yusuf, Isabella Guimet Pedraz, Harry Chen, David Macababayao and Kevin Sunkwa-Mills
